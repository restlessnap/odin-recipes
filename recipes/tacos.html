Tacos

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a corn or wheat tortilla folded or rolled around a filling.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Heat the tortillas in a skillet over medium-high heat until they are warm and slightly crispy.
  2. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until it is browned and cooked through.
  3. Add the tomato sauce, cheese, lettuce, onion, cilantro, and lime to the skillet and cook until the cheese is melted and the ingredients are heated through.
  4. Fold the tortillas in half to make tacos and serve.
