Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Olives
  • Peppers
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine pizza dough, tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, olives, peppers, and garlic.
  3. Bake at 450 degrees F (230 degrees C) for 15 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
